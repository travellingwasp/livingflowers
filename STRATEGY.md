# Strategy

## Selected Positioning

WindowPlant Lab helps renters match apartment window conditions to houseplant placement using practical tools, checklists, and clearly labeled assumptions.

## Why This Can Win Impressions Within 30 Days

- Long-tail queries are specific: "north facing window plants apartment", "how far from window for plants", and "plant light by window direction".
- Search results often mix generic plant lists with incomplete placement advice.
- Original tools can earn clicks from utility-oriented snippets and referrers.
- Content risk is low if the site avoids fabricated testing and labels assumptions.

## Initial Bottleneck

Discovery and indexing. The first week should prioritize clean deployment, Search Console verification, sitemap submission, crawlability, and a small number of strong utility pages.

## 30-Day Operating Bias

Prefer improving demonstrated opportunities over publishing new content:

1. Indexing and technical SEO.
2. Pages with impressions but no clicks.
3. Pages with visits but weak engagement.
4. Tools, templates, or datasets with a clear query target.
5. Legitimate distribution in relevant communities only when it directly helps users.
