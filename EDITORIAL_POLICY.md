# Editorial Policy

## Publishing Standard

Every published page must solve a real user problem. Every substantial page must include at least one original useful component:

- Calculator.
- Checklist.
- Downloadable template.
- Comparison table.
- Decision tree.
- Original diagram.
- Practical process.
- Curated dataset.
- Interactive utility.
- Original analysis.

## Prohibited Content Practices

Do not fabricate:

- Personal experience.
- Expert credentials.
- Reviews.
- Tests.
- Quotes.
- Statistics.
- Customer stories.
- Author biographies.

## Evidence and Sources

- Use reliable sources when making factual claims.
- Label assumptions.
- Prefer project-created diagrams, charts, screenshots, and datasets.
- Record licensed image attribution before use.

## Improvement Rule

Pages with evidence of impressions, clicks, visits, or referrers get priority over new pages unless the bottleneck is indexing, analytics, or a missing core utility.
